Snake Game Arcade

Overview

Welcome to the classic Snake Game — a timeless favorite where your reflexes are tested as you grow your snake and avoid crashing into yourself. It’s easy to learn and addictive to master.

How to Play

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control the snake. Eat the green dots to grow longer. Avoid hitting the walls or your own tail!

Tips for Success

  • Plan your moves ahead of time.
  • Stick to the edges when the snake is short.
  • Turn slowly — avoid sudden direction changes!
